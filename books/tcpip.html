OSI reference model layer... General Things

The OSI refrence model is just used as a refernce, for people to have a general idea to compare to when talking about a netowork or web app. They're two diffrent parts of the model. The lower bits and the upper part. The lower bits conist of the pysical, data link, network and transport. the upper part conist of session, presintation, and Application. A message is generally sent through a arch.
For example, a message could be sent through the Application layer(7) and it would go through the presendation, sesion, or etc until it reached the other machine. Then, it would go back up the OSI refrence 1-7. Or in a u shape if both stacks were next to each other.

Layer 1: Physical Layer

Generally anything, that says Physical Layer, means that it repersentsthe physical parts of a computer. The layer conists of cables, connectors, wireless radio transceivers, etc. anything that show raw bits. Addtionally, transforms the bits into data that can be sent throught the network. After the transforming, or encoding, then transmits it. Applies to both wired and wireless.
